    • FREQUENT ASKED QUESTIONS


    Your ultimate destination for all things comics, manga, and more! At Satoshi, we are passionate about bringing the best stories from around the world to your fingertips. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a new fan, our store offers a diverse selection of comics, manga, light novels, books, figures, games and merchandise.


    What types of comics do you carry?

    We have a wide selection of modern comics from all the major publishers like Marvel, DC, Image, Dark Horse and more. We also have an extensive collection of vintage comics dating back to the Golden Age.


    Do you buyback or trade comics?

    Yes, we buy and trade comic books, graphic novels, collectibles and other pop culture items. Get cash or store credit when you bring in your collections.


    Do you have a comic subscription service?

    Absolutely! Our pull list subscription service ensures you'll never miss an issue of your favorite ongoing comic series. We'll automatically set aside the books for you each week.


    Can I place special orders for hard to find items?

    You bet. We're happy to use our distributor connections to try to track down any out-of-print or hard to find comic books, figures, statues and other collectibles you're looking for.


    Do you host any events?

    We sure do! Check our events calendar for the latest details on our monthly comic book swaps, artist signings, cosplay contests and more.


    What are your hours?

    We're open 7 days a week: Monday-Saturday 10am-9pm, Sundays 11am-6pm. Stop in anytime!
