    Welcome to Satoshi


    Your ultimate destination for all things comics, manga, and more! At Satoshi, we are passionate about bringing the best stories from around the world to your fingertips. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a new fan, our store offers a diverse selection of comics, manga, light novels, books, figures, and merchandise.


    Our Story

    Satoshi was founded by a group of comic enthusiasts who believe in the power of storytelling. We started as a small shop with a big dream: to create a haven for fans of every genre and style. Today, we are proud to serve a vibrant community of readers, collectors, and fans who share our love for the art and magic of comics.


    What We Offer

    • Comics: From classic superhero tales to modern graphic novels, our comic collection spans every universe and genre.
    • Manga: Dive into the world of Japanese manga with our extensive selection, featuring popular titles and hidden gems.
    • Light Novels: Explore captivating stories with our range of light novels, perfect for those who love immersive reads.
    • Books: Discover a curated collection of books that complement our comic and manga offerings, including art books and novels.
    • Figures: Bring your favorite characters to life with our high-quality figures, perfect for display and collection.
    • Merchandise: Show off your fandom with our wide array of merchandise, from keychains and wallets to posters and apparel.
    • Games: Enjoy a variety of board games and card games that feature beloved characters and themes.

    Our Mission

    At Satoshi, we are dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive space for all fans. Our mission is to connect people through the love of storytelling and to support the comic and manga community by offering the best products and services.

    Thank you for choosing Satoshi as your go-to store for all your comic and manga needs. We are excited to share our passion with you and help you discover your next favorite story!


    Visit Us

    Come visit our store or browse our online catalog to find your next great read or collectible. At Satoshi, every story matters, and we can't wait to be part of your adventure.
